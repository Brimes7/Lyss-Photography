Hello, I’m Lyssa Faith and I’m a photographer based in Southeast New Mexico. I have lived in the Lovington and Hobbs area my whole life. My passion is to tell a story with each photo. Capturing the realness of emotions drives me to pursue this passion.

Trying new Starbucks drinks daily is an addiction of mine, along with creating and editing videos. Black is my favorite color. Shopping or scrolling through making a wishlist makes my soul happy.

I have interned under talented photographers that have allowed me to hone my talents while also growing my understanding of the best techniques. I have produced, edited and directed the news at Lovington High school and in y free time I work on short films.

My plan is to go straight into film school once I graduate highschool in 2022. The options of film schools are endless, but NYU Film will forever be at the top of my list! No matter where I end up, the photo opportunities in different parts of the country will be limitless!

I love being around my friends and family, hanging out and watching movies. Work and school take up most of my time. Every chance I get, I find a way to spend time with the ones who never left my side and believed in me when I didn’t even believe in myself.
